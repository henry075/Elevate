PROGRAM

Program 1: Youth Mental Wellness Objective: To provide comprehensive psychosocial support to children and youth, promoting mental health and emotional well-being.

Program 2: Youth for Climate Action Objective: To engage and empower youth in climate change mitigation and adaptation efforts, fostering environmental stewardship and sustainable practices.

Program 3: Food Security and Youth Empowerment Objective: To enhance food security through sustainable agricultural practices and empower youth with skills in agribusiness and food production.

ABOUT

At Elevate the Young, we are dedicated to fostering the growth and development of youth through a multifaceted approach that addresses mental health, environmental stewardship, and food security. Our mission is to empower young people by providing comprehensive psychosocial support, engaging them in climate change mitigation and adaptation efforts, and equipping them with the skills needed for sustainable agricultural practices.

Our programs are designed to create a holistic impact. We offer counseling, therapy, and educational workshops to promote mental health and emotional well-being among children and youth. Our school-based programs and awareness campaigns aim to destigmatize mental health issues and provide support for caregivers and educators.

Through climate education workshops, community green projects, and advocacy training, we engage youth in proactive environmental stewardship. Our initiatives promote sustainable practices and empower young leaders to influence climate policies and drive positive change in their communities.

We enhance food security by teaching sustainable agricultural techniques and supporting youth-led agribusiness startups. Our school and community gardens, nutrition education, and advocacy efforts aim to improve nutrition and promote sustainable food systems.

Together, we envision a resilient and thriving community of youth who are mentally and emotionally healthy, actively engaged in combating climate change, and leading efforts towards sustainable food security and agribusiness innovation. Join us in our journey to create a better, more sustainable future for all.
